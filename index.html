DigiPunks Project

About DigiPunks

DigitalPunks is a 1000 NFTs collection which will take over the metaverse! Each Punks has his story, living on new city called DigitalCity & Can participate to races game!

DigitalPunks is on-chain Racing game, Integration of metaverse, DigitalPunks Free airdrop.

$DigiCoin Utility token

Burn $DigiCoin to Mint your Race Car, Enter races, Upgrade your cars and bet on races!

How it works?

We would have a minimum and maximum requirement for the race cars in-terms of attributes across different leagues so its fair for everyone.

You can race privately with friends and bet as much $DigiCoin as you want and the winner takes all!

You will need 2 Punk Races + 1 Car to start playing & collecting Tokens
